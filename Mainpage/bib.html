Existing online platforms to make educational videos interactive

  • Vialogue: Time Stamped comments for discussion as a discussion forum.
  • VideoNote.es: Take time-stamped notes. Can Save on Google Drive and re-open. Click to play part synched with video.
  • EdPuzzle: Insert quiz (comment, multiple choice or open-ended) inside the video.
  • TedEd: Attach Multiple choice/ Open-ended question to vide, Discussion Forum / or Comments outside of video
  • EduCanon: Insert questions (multiple choice, select-all-that-apply etc) inside the video. Give answer.
  • HapYak: Insert pop-up quiz within video, annotate video with image and drawing on top of it.
  • Blubbr: Insert multiple choice questions within video, give immediate feedback.
