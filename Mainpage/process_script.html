1. Divide Transcript into slides
    • Given a sentence, s_begin, s_end, slide_begin and slide_end following cases can happen:
      1. Sentence begins and ends before slide segment
        sentence_begin < sentence_end < slide_begin < slide_end: Do not include
      2. Sentence begins and ends after slide segment
        slide_begin < slide_end < sentence_begin < sentence_end: Do not include
      3. Sentence entirely contained within slide segment
        slide_begin < sentence_begin < sentence_end < slide_end: Include
      4. Sentence begins before slide segment, ends within slide segment
        sentence_begin < slide_begin < sentence_end < slide_end: Do not include
      5. Sentence begins within slide segment and ends after
        slide_begin < sentence_begin < slide_end < sentence_end: Include
    • Include all sentences that begin at or after (greater than or equal to) slide segment begins, and ends before or at the end of the slide segment.
